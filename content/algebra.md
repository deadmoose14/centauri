### Field Axioms

Let $a,b,c \in \mathbb{R}$

Property | Addition | Multiplication
---------|----------|----------------
Associativity|$$a+(b+c)=(a+b)+c$$|$$a(bc)=(ab)c$$
Commutativity|$$a+b=b+a$$|$$ab=ba$$
Inverse|$$a+(-a)=0$$|$$a\times\frac{1}{a} = 1$$
Identity|$$a+0=a$$|$$a\times 1 = a$$

### Distribution 
$a(b+c)=ab+ac$
