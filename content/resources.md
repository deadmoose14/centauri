## Arithmetic Rules
**Def:** A **complex number** is an ordered pair $(a,b)$ where $a,b \in \mathbb{R}$, is written as $a+bi$.

**Def:** The set of all complex numbers, denoted as $\mathbb{C}$:

$\mathbb{C} = {a+bi : a,b \in \mathbb{C}}$
